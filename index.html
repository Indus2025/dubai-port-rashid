    ABOUT RASHID YACHTS & MARINA

    Mina Rashid, also known as Rashid Yachts & Marina, is a premier waterfront destination developed by Emaar Properties. This historic port has been transformed into a luxurious residential community, offering a harmonious blend of modern living and rich cultural heritage.


    Strategically located along Dubai's coastline, Mina Rashid provides residents with unparalleled views of the marina and the Arabian Gulf. The community features a diverse range of properties, including contemporary apartments and townhouses, designed to cater to various lifestyles and preferences.


    Residents can enjoy a plethora of amenities such as a state-of-the-art marina, waterfront dining and retail outlets, lush green parks, and access to a private beach, ensuring a vibrant and fulfilling lifestyle.
